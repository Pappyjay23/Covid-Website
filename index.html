What is Coronavirus

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a virus transmitted through droplets generated when an infected person coughs, sneezes or exhales.These droplets are too heavy to hsng in the air and fall on the floor or surfaces. You can be infected by:

Breathing in the virus

Touching a contaminated surface

Using a contaminated object

Stay at Home

Stay at home often to avoid coming in contact with an infected person.

Wear Facemask

Make sure to always use a facemask when going out as this will prevent the spread of the virus

Keep Social Distancing

Make sure to keep your distance when relating with people to avoid touching an infected person.

Wash your hands

Regularly wash your hands with soap and water to prevent the virus.

How to prevent Coronavirus

The Coronavirus can be prevented by doing the following:

Avoid touching surfaces

Clean surfaces with disinfectants

Frequently clean hands with soap and water.

Ensure to use facemask

Symptoms of Coronavirus

The following are symptoms of the Coronavirus

High Fever

This is a temporary increase in the average temperature of a person which is 37 degree celcius.

Sore throat

This is the pain or irritation in the throat that can occur with or without swallowing.

Cough

This is a persistent hacking sound to release air and clear irritation in the throat or airway.

Headache

This is a painful sensation in any part of the head, ranging from sharp to dull.

Stay at home and call your doctor: If any of these symptoms are noticed, make sure to contact your doctor.
